About Skywatcher

Skywatcher is a lightweight, no-framework weather app built with vanilla JS and the OpenWeatherMap API. It provides real-time conditions, 5-day forecasts, and live weather map layers.

🌡️Current ConditionsTemp, humidity, wind, pressure & more
📅5-Day ForecastHourly-grouped daily predictions
🗺️Live Map LayersWind, clouds, temp & pressure tiles
